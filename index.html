About Me

I'm Stephen! I'm a passionate Product Manager who loves creating solutions that bring people and data together via software, solving problems that resonate with users and make everyday life easier and more enjoyable.

My knowledge and experience have helped me develop a deep understanding of user behaviors and needs and what it takes to create rich and lasting customer relations. These understandings help me to envision both current needs and future needs. Allowing me to scope, prioritize, and execute the solutions that will significantly impact users.

Outside of work, I am often exploring the outdoors, traveling to new places, or finding ways to help my local community. I love new challenges, new experiences, and helping others. Want to connect? Feel free to reach out!
