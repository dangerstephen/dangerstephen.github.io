 Vagabond 

This app was built using Ruby on Rails during a 2 day sprint. Users have the ability to create blog posts in cities they have traveled too, so other users know just what to do when in that city themselves. Not sure the place looks like what its hyped up to be? You don't even have to leave the page to check with Google Maps intergrated in you can explore the posted location all within the post. Go and learn about what your going to do in your next city.
