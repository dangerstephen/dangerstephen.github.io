 Postmates & Flock 

During the course of 30 hours at the Developer Week 2017 Hackaton I built 2 fully functional Flock OS apps. The first is a basic widget that upon a the slash comand of '/postmates' a model pops up inside the chat allowing you to order food for your team. The second app was an additon onto that. Dont want to decide what to eat? Problem solved use the '/order' command and the user will be sent to a bot to help order from any of the top 5 nearest places.

To get started with either of these download them and get ordering! Postmates Flock Widget. & Postmates Flock Bot.
